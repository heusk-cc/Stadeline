Senior High School Strands

Choose your pathway toward university readiness and specialized professional training.

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics

Designed for students aspiring to pursue careers in engineering, pure sciences, medical fields, information systems, and technical research applications.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management

Concentrates on corporate operations, financial analytics, institutional management, accounting foundational frameworks, and entrepreneurial studies.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences

Tailored for students focused on development sectors, communication sciences, legal pathways, political history, sociology, and journalism careers.

GAS

General Academic Strand

Provides a flexible curriculum framework curated for students exploring multi-disciplinary fields, offering a balanced combination of humanities, business, and social science electives.

HE

Home Economics

Focuses on developing practical, globally competitive proficiencies in culinary arts, hospitality operations, tourism management, and commercial food services.

ICT

Information and Communications Technology

Empowers students with advanced digital proficiencies in software programming, system operations, database management, web architecture, and networking essentials.
